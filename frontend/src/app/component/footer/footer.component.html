About Us The founder of Prade Jewels Deepthi S R Prabhu, chanced upon the idea of connecting the jewelry enthusiasts to the proficient artisans across the globe and channeled her dream through Prade. Her choice of unique jewelry, intrinsic designs, understanding of alloys, ability to cater to client taste is what sets PraDe apart from the rest of the Market. With humble beginnings from an in house store to now supplying the products across the Globe through various mediums like stores, digital media, social media and many endorsements in a very short span of time is the true motivation for the company to up the goals and only steer forward. The motive was hence fueled right and now there is a huge road map waiting for Prade to conquer.
